Where Code Meets Calm

I’m currently working on a startup called MadGlow — a project that brings together skincare, technology, and creativity. It’s a mix of everything I love: problem-solving, design, and making ideas come to life.

Our team has been developing an app that helps people track their skincare journey and learn what works best for them. Along the way, I’ve been learning about both the technical side of app development and the marketing side of building a brand from scratch.

  • Working on app development and testing features
  • Designing a clean, easy-to-use interface
  • Creating marketing content and building a small online community

It’s been a fun challenge — turning an idea into something real, one late-night brainstorming session at a time.
